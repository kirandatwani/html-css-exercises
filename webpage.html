More on Japan

Japan is an island country made up of 4 main islands (Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu) and a few thousand smaller islands. Located in East Asia, it's a popular destination for many adventurous tourists. Japan is a home for sushi, anime and traditional arts such as tea ceremonies, flower-arranging and calligraphy.

Study Abroad

I studied abroad in Kobe, Japan in Spring 2020 through the Study Abroad Center at UH Manoa. Studying abroad is a great opportunity and experience, and I definitely recommend it if you are able to fit it into your college schedule. There are several countries to travel to, languages to immerse in, and subjects to learn along the way!
